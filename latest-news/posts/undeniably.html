MY REVIEW

Undeniably by Cala Riley
My rating: 4 of 5 stars




   


Another brilliant read by one of my favorite authors- Cala Riley. From the moment I started the Brighton academy series , I absolutely adored Keaton. He is sweet enough for anyone to love. This was the third book of the series and was of the youngest of the three siblings.
Our beloved Keaton finally reconnects with his long lost love- Morgan. The things in the starting were tense between them, but later they smoothed out. I won't give away too much spoilers because this book is a must read for all!!!



I absolutely loved the way sage held the family together. The way she cared for the whole family was just so sweet and nice.
Morgan- the main character of the book , was at times a bit immature for me. I mean I understood her actions but sometimes she just seemed unreasonable. But in the end, I guess all of Keaton's cuteness (I really couldn't find a more appropriate word!!) made the book even more enjoyable!!
This book is recommended to all the slow-burn, second-chance romance lovers!!

AUTHOR BIO


Cala Riley, better known as Cala and Riley, are a pair of friends with a deep seated love of books and
writing. Both Cala and Riley are happily married and each have children, Cala with the four-legged
kind while Riley has a mixture of both two-legged and four. While they live apart, that does not
affect their connection. They are the true definition of family. What started as an idea that quickly
turned into a full-length book and a bond that will never end.
