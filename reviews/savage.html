SAVAGE

- TRACY LORRAINE

Savage by Tracy Lorraine
My rating: 5 of 5 stars





From the moment Paine-Mason and Camile's book ended , I have been waiting for Ethan's book.This book certainly made the wait worth it!!
The first book of Tracy Lorraine that I read was THORN and from then I have been following her work. Just like all of hers books this one also does not disappoint.
In the starting of the book I absolutely hated Ethan, What an ass he was !! and it was absolutely unreasonable. My heart got pulled towards Rae and I sympathised with her so much!!
But as the book progressed, just like all of the Anti-heroes of Tracy Lorraine, this one too won my heart.
Ethan and Rae's story is not easy at all. There were times when I was disappointed in Rae because of how rude she was to people who did not deserve it , but then i completely got where she was coming from and then fell in love with her even more!!
her courage and strength was absolutely mind-blowing!! and I just loved the way she gave back as good as she got from Ethan!! she was truly the most strongest female character of Tracy Lorraine .
I totally recommend this book to all the bully romance lovers!!!
