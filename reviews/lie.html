LIE

- PENELOPE SKY

Lie by Penelope Sky
My rating: 4 of 5 stars





I had this book on pre-order, pretty since the day the option came. And it finally got released!!!!!
This is the story of Heath, who is Balto's brother. You may have met him in the skull king trilogy, i.e. Balto's trilogy.
Both Catalina and Heath were perfect for each other. The best thing I find about Penelope's books are those unexpected twist and turns. In the starting of the book, it started pretty light, but as the book progressed, Damn!! that was some entertainment!
The way she writes her books is amazing. I just love all of the heroes of her book. The way she manipulates the story line is worth reading for!
So in this book , we get to meet the characters of the skull king trilogy again, and also Heath and Catalina.
My heart just hurt so much for Catalina. The way she cared for everyone was so selfless (even for a certain asshole-family-member that didn't deserve it!!). Both of them start off a bit rough, and then things smooth out in the middle. But then the ending came, and it nearly broke my heart. The way Heath understood Catalina was so amazing, Their connection nearly drove me crazy. I just can't wait for the second book in this series. The final book of both Heath and Catalina is going to be amazing!!!
