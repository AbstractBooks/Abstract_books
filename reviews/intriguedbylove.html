INTRIGUED BY LOVE

- SEINNA SNOW

Intrigued By Love by Sienna Snow
My rating: 4 of 5 stars






This was the fist book I have read by this author!! and let me tell you, she just made to my top 10 authors!! yes, this book was that good. I completely loved the dynamic between Jax and our heroine. They both were perfect for each other.
There were times when I wanted to smack them for being to stupid and impulsive!! they just wasted so much time. But then everything worked out for the best in the end. The way the story was portrayed by the author was very good, I just felt attached to the characters in the first few chapters of the book!!!
this book is recommended to all the contemporary romance lovers with a little tinge of kink in the scenes!!!
