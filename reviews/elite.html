ELITE

- RACHEL VAN DYKEN

Elite by Rachel Van Dyken
My rating: 5 of 5 stars




This book just blew my mind!!!
This is the first Rachel van Dyken i have read and lets just say that i will be adding all of her books to my lists, that's how good it was.
the way she wrote it, the characters and the glimpses she gave us of the upcoming couples , everything was perfect.



The best thing i found in this book was the way the characters found such profound happiness , even after so much shit happened. We all read in the book that Nixon was ,by no way, easy on Trace. The way he bullied in the start!! he was an absolute ass!!
But the characters, more importantly Trace still found it in her to love him and him her.
I am just so excited about the other books!!!!
