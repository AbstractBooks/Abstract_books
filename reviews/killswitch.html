KILL SWITCH

- PENELOPE DOUGLAS

Kill Switch by Penelope Douglas
My rating: 5 of 5 stars





IT'S FINALLY HERE!!!!!!!
Total rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐


i had this books on preorder pretty much from the day when the preorder link came😂😂!!
From the moment I read the excerpt in hideaway, i have been waiting for this novel.
As much as i loved Damon as a Villian, I absolutely have no words for him as a hero. He was amazing!!
when I first read corrupt, I was like, there is no way Damon will get a book!!! but then i saw his Except and his scenes in hideaway and was finally convinced that he was worth redemption.


The best part of this Book was the scenes between damon and Winter when they were younger. Their Chemistry was off the charts and i was just left begging for more of their scenes when the book finally ended!!

And don't even have me talk about Damon and Will!! I dont think i have still recovered from their scene. It was certainly unexpected!! But worth reading.
this book is for every person who would love to see a former villain redeem himself and also for the bully romance category lovers!!!
