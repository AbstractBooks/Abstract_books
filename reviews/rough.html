ROUGH

- RENEE ROSE & VANESSA VALE

Rough by Renee Rose
My rating: 4 of 5 stars





One of the biggest reasons why I like and read renee's books are is, that she never makes me wait too long for the next part and of course because she writes amazing kinky reads!!I have never read cowboy type books before and i must say that this one did not disappoint me at all. The story line was good and the characters were good. This book was a pretty simple read for me and the story was rather short. Here we meet audrey and boyd , where boyd is a sort of cowboy in his place and audrey is a doctor. their chemistry was off the charts and the scenes were very good. I really enjoyed this book as a short steamy read.
This book is recommended to all the contemporary romance lovers with a touch of kink in there steamy scenes.
