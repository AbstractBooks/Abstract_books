VICIOUS

- LJ SHEN

Vicious by L.J. Shen
My rating: 4 of 5 stars





Total stars: - 4 HUGE STARS
Ratings:-
Hero: 5/5
Heroine: 4/5
Drama: 4/5
Romance:4.5/5
Suspense: 3.5/5
Agony: 4/5
Sexual scenes: 4/5
I am such a huge fan of bully romance and I have lost count of the no of bully books I have read. Vicious literally topped all of my lists of bully romance books.

I am absolutely in love with vicious and Emilia. Their chemistry was off the charts and they were absolutely entertaining. Vicious was so ruthless and hot, my god I absolutely loved him.
There were a few scenes that made me cry so hard, ohh god!!!

However, it was the ending that took my heart. you see in many books the female protagonist is a literal doormat and just forgives the hero for anything he does, which just irritates me to no end. But that was not the case in this book. Emilia made vicious work hard for his fuckups and that was absolutely entertaining and satisfying to read. Moreover, I would also like to applaud our hero for not giving up on our pink haired heroine.
