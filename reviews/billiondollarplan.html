BILLION DOLLAR PLAN

- THANDIWE MPOFU

The Billion Dollar Plan by Thandiwe Mpofu
My rating: 5 of 5 stars





I have been a huge fan of Thandiwe Mpofu books from the moment I read the Westbrook series. As soon as I finished those series , I quickly picked up these series because I knew that these books will be amazing!!! And guess what?
I WAS RIGHT!!!!
This book was just as amazing as reckless hate. I absolutely loved the angst and the painful dynamic between Chloe and Gideon.
There chemistry was off the charts hot!
Both of the main characters had one motive throughout the book. Revenge. They both wanted people to suffer for whatever they did which made them both hurt. I absolutely loved how ruthless they both were in that regard!!
This thrilling read had me hooked right from the start!! This book is recommended to all the suspense-thriller-romance lovers !! This book is for you and you won't regret reading it!!! 😍😍💓💓💓
