BE MY BRAYSHAW

- MEAGAN BRANDY

Be My Brayshaw by Meagan Brandy
My rating: 5 of 5 stars





Victoria and Captain are here!!! From the moment I read that teaser of this book at the end of the Brayshaw trilogy, jesus!!! only I know how much I dreamed of having this book there and then!!!
The way the story is written by Meagan brandy is just marvelous.
Both Captain and Victoria just came in and blew my world. I didn't know that Captain had it in him to be such an asshole sometimes!! Those were some interesting scenes.



The way Captain fought for his attraction to Victoria and those moments of his vulnerability with Zoey just broke my heart. The way Zoey and the Brayshaws relationship is portrayed in this book can reduce anyone to tears.
According to me Zoey as the glue that kept the family together. Captain tried so damn hard to not give in to his attraction to Victoria, But damn that girl was stubborn. No matter what he did, she somehow broke down all his walls and ended up being his in the end.
The ending of this book just blew my mind. I lost count of the moments where I just wanted to Slap Captain so bad. If only he had trusted Victoria and heard her first. So much shit-storm could have been pretended. Victoria had so many secrets of her own. This whole book was basically based on her unveiling those secrets. The way those twists came was just perfect.




The most perfect relationship that I found out in this book was that of Zoey and Victoria. Both of them were connected in a way that no matter how much you try to break, it just couldn't be broken. They were just so cuteee!!!

“Ice cream!” she shouts and runs away, but not before pausing and running back to rub her nose across mine. “Eskimo kisses!” I force the tears in my eyes to stay right where they are. “And butterfly hugs.” She laughs and runs away. “So much, Rora!” So much, ZoZo.

That ending was just fabulous. The emotions were high and the angst between the characters was just so perfect. Then there was that small teaser for Royce's book. I mean it's like the author doesn't want me to sleep peacefully. Now i'll spent the time left in dreaming about Royce's book!!!!
This is a book that none of you want to miss!! Its a must read for all!!!
