BEAUTIFULLY CRUEL

- J.T. GEISSINGER

Beautifully Cruel by J.T. Geissinger
My rating: 5 of 5 stars





This was probably my 5-6 book by this author and as always she has blown my mind with her way with the story line , the humor and those witty characters!!



The first thing that I would like to appreciate about this book is the humor in this book!!
my god!! I have never seen a book as humorous as this one! the female protagonist of this book simply stole my heart with her easy way with words and sexy comebacks. Tru was literally everything I aspire to be, from her sexy mouth to her appearance, she was a true queen!
Then there was Liam. The blurb of this book says that Liam is dominant, and believe me , that sentence is 100% true!! He is that perfect mixture of dominant and sexy and I was drooling after him for nearly 75%of the book.
The twists in this book were so unexpected and the ending was perfect!!
