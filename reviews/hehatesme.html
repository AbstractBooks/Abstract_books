HE HATES ME

- RINA KENT

He Hates Me by Rina Kent
My rating: 4 of 5 stars




Just like all rina's books, this one is also perfect and not disappointing at all!!!!
I have been for this release for a long time and it was soooooo worth the wait.
This was my first stalker romance and I must say I really liked this one!!
My first thought when I picked up this book was that "this stalker romance sounds cringey" , but let me tell you that the way the author has written it , it was anything but cringy.
The scenes were well appreciated by me and I just can't wait for book 2!!!!!!!!!!!
Let me also warn you that this is literally a stalker romance.. I mean there is the "stuff-being-moved-everywhere" without the heroines permission and the window peaking. And everything u can imagine a stalker would do... But none the less , I never thought of that as a negative part of the book ... I mean it's stalker romance! What else did you expect????
