BOHEMIAN PATIENCE

- MISTY WALKER

Bohemian Patience by Misty Walker
My rating: 4 of 5 stars






This was a quite simple book for me. The story line of the book was borderline sweet and the characters were perfect in their roles. This was the first book I have read by Misty walker and I must say that I am impressed.
Freedom was the perfect hero of a book. He was everything in one hot package. His kindness and thoughtfulness were the things that I found the most attractive things about him. The moment he meets Essie, he knows he has found his match.

Essie was a bit complicated character. From her childhood she had been told that she had a dark soul and her only saving grace was her grandmother who kept her pretty much secluded from the outside world. When she meets Freedom, she knows that this her chance to escape her prison and both of them embark on a journey filled with love and passion. The humorous parts of the book made me laugh so hard.
I am going to keep this review short and sweet and will just recommend this to all the contemporary romance lovers !!!
