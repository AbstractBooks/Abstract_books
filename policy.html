Not all review requests are going to be accepted.

The only places that I most a review in on my Goodreads, Bookbub and My blog. Currently there is some problem with my Amazon account and I don't post reviews there. The mechanism I keep in mind while giving the star rating is as follows:

5⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

I really like this book and will recommend it anyone and everyone I come across. It is a re-read type of book, and I will definitely re-read it!

4⭐⭐⭐⭐

This book is one of my favorites and will also recommend this book to everyone! I will try to re-read it if I get the time.

3⭐⭐⭐

This book was a one time read for me and was the OK type. I will recommend it to very few people, specifically those who are a fan of that author or a fan of that particular book category!

2⭐⭐

I just could not connect to this book! I did read the book completely but won't recommend this anyone!

1⭐

This was the worst book for me! the story line of the book went completely over my head and I would never ever recommend this to anyone. 

THIS IS MY POLICY. THERE MAY BE PEOPLE WHO WILL LIKE THE 1⭐ RATED BOOKS OF MINE. BUT THAT'S OK ,BECAUSE EVERYBODY HAS A DIFFERENT TASTE!
